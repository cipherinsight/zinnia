=== diagnosis ===
grayscott issues 8 `require_static_int` queries during compile.
All 8 resolve via the static_val fast-path; ZERO of them reach the
range layer or the SMT layer. Therefore turning SMT on or off cannot
change correctness or coverage on this benchmark — the spec's claim
that grayscott "flips TIMEOUT→PASS" because of the SMT default cannot
be true: SMT never runs for this benchmark.

The 28s compile time is wall-clock noise / actual compile work; SMT
contributes 0.0ms. The historical baseline p3_layered_on.json claim
of `1284s` for grayscott is implausible against the current code path
and is most likely a one-time artifact of process-pool contention.
